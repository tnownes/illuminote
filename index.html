Illuminote Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 8, 2026

Summary

Illuminote stores your data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or sell personal data.

Data We Handle

  • Journal entries and reflections you create
  • Optional profile preferences (e.g., session length, reminders)
  • Optional speech-to-text input (processed on-device)

Data Collection and Sharing

We do not collect or share your data with third parties. We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

Permissions

  • Microphone and Speech Recognition: Used only if you enable speech-to-text.
  • Notifications: Used only if you enable reminders.
